AUTOFLOW-WELL AQUFER MONITORING SYSTEM




At PARvaah, we are committed to transforming the way autoflow wells are managed, particularly in regions like the Terai of India. These unique wells harness naturally pressurized aquifers to provide essential water resources but often suffer from challenges like uncontrolled flow and groundwater wastage. Our innovative solution integrates advanced mechanical systems, sensor-driven controls, and cutting-edge AI/ML technologies to optimize well performance. By employing tools like venturi meters, we ensure precise monitoring and management of water flow, reducing overflow and conserving groundwater. With a focus on sustainability and efficiency, Pravaah is dedicated to ensuring a reliable water supply while protecting this vital natural resource for future generations. Join us in our mission to make water management smarter and more sustainable!

About Parvaah

Welcome to Parvaah, an initiative designed to revolutionize water management through technology. Our platform leverages IoT sensors and data analytics to monitor water flow, depth, pressure, and quality in real time, ensuring sustainable water usage and proactive management.

At Parvaah, we believe in using technology for the betterment of communities, helping solve critical water-related challenges. By providing insights into water usage, we aim to contribute to efficient water conservation and management practices.

Our Features

  • Real-time Monitoring: Track water flow, pressure, and quality with live data.
  • Predictive Analytics: Use AI to predict flow states and manage resources efficiently.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Access data through an intuitive web platform.
  • Sustainability: Promote efficient water usage and reduce wastage.

Our Mission

To empower communities with cutting-edge technology to conserve and manage water resources efficiently, ensuring a sustainable future for generations to come.
