ABOUT US

At Pravaah, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the management of autoflow wells in regions like the Terai of India. These wells tap into naturally pressurized aquifers, offering a vital water source but often leading to uncontrolled water flow and groundwater wastage. Our integrated solution combines advanced mechanical systems, sensor-based control, and AI/ML technologies to optimize well performance, minimize overflow, and ensure sustainable water management. By utilizing tools like venturi meters, we aim to improve accuracy and efficiency, ensuring a reliable water supply while conserving valuable groundwater resources.
